Privacy Policy

Application: True Forward  |  Developer: RB Creative Solutions LLC  |  Effective date: March 2, 2026

1. Overview

True Forward is a Gmail Add-on that forwards emails using your own email address as the sender, rather than re-sending from the original sender's address. It is designed for use with services such as QuickBooks that identify accounts by the sender's email address.

True Forward runs entirely within your Google Account using Google Apps Script. RB Creative Solutions LLC does not operate any external servers, does not receive your email content, and does not collect, store, or process any of your personal data.

2. Data Accessed

True Forward accesses the following data within your Google Account:

  • Gmail messages — to read emails you have labeled for auto-forwarding and to send forwarded copies from your address.
  • Gmail labels — to apply a "/sent" suffix label after a message is successfully forwarded and archived.
  • Your email address — to identify you as the sender when forwarding messages.
  • Google Sheets (optional) — if you have configured a log sheet ID in settings, activity logs are written to a spreadsheet in your own Google Drive.

3. Data Stored

True Forward stores your configuration (forward presets and auto-forward label rules) using Google's PropertiesService, which stores data within your Google Account. This data never leaves Google's infrastructure and is never transmitted to or accessed by RB Creative Solutions LLC.

No cookies, analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party SDKs are used by this add-on.

4. Data Sharing

When True Forward forwards an email, it sends that email to the address(es) you have specified in your forwarding rules. This is the core function of the add-on and occurs only at your explicit direction. No email content is shared with RB Creative Solutions LLC or any other party.

5. Google API Scopes Used

True Forward requests the following OAuth scopes and uses each only as described:

Scope Purpose
gmail.send Send forwarded emails from your address.
gmail.modify Read labeled emails, apply "/sent" labels, and archive processed messages.
gmail.addons.execute Required by Google for all Gmail Add-ons to run in the sidebar.
script.scriptapp Create and manage the hourly time-based trigger that processes your label rules automatically.
userinfo.email Display your email address in the add-on and set it as the "From" address on forwarded emails.
spreadsheets Write activity logs to a Google Sheet in your Drive, if you have configured a log sheet (optional).

True Forward's use of Google user data adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Data Retention

Your forwarding configuration is retained in Google PropertiesService until you delete your rules within the add-on or uninstall the add-on. Uninstalling the add-on from your Google Account removes all associated stored properties. No data is retained by RB Creative Solutions LLC.

7. Children's Privacy

True Forward is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
