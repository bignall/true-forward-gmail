True Forward

A Gmail Add-on that forwards emails from your own address, with all attachments included.

View on GitHub Setup Guide

Why True Forward?

Services like QuickBooks, expense trackers, and CRMs identify your account by the sender's email address. Gmail's built-in forward sends the original email as-is — it arrives from the original sender, not you. True Forward composes a brand-new email from your address so the receiving service matches it to your account.

Features

One-Click Forward

Click a preset button in the Gmail sidebar to forward instantly.

Preset Destinations

Save frequently used addresses and forward with a single click.

Auto-Forward by Label

Apply a Gmail label and True Forward handles the rest automatically every hour.

All Attachments Included

PDFs, images, inline content — everything comes along for the ride.

Audit Log

Every forward is logged to a Google Sheet in your own Drive.

Privacy First

No external servers. All data stays in your Google account.

How It Works

1

Open an Email

Select any email in Gmail. True Forward appears in the sidebar.

2

Pick a Destination

Click a saved preset or type a custom address.

3

Sent From You

A new email is composed from your address with the full original content and attachments.

Quick Setup

  1. Go to script.google.com and create a new project
  2. Copy the .gs files from the src/ folder into your project
  3. Replace appsscript.json with the one from the repo
  4. Deploy → Test deployments → Gmail Add-on → Install
  5. Open any email in Gmail — True Forward appears in the right sidebar
